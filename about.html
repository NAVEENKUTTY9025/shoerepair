Our Legacy

A Family Tradition of Excellence

Founded in Milan in 1987 by master craftsman Giuseppe Romano, SoleRestore began as a small atelier dedicated to the art of shoe repair. Today, we've evolved into a premium mobile service while preserving the same traditional techniques passed down through generations.

Every restoration is handled with the same care and attention to detail that defined our founder's work. We believe that quality footwear deserves expert care, and we bring that expertise directly to your door with our convenient mobile service.

— Giuseppe Romano, Founder
