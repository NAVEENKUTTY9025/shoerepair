About This Service

Our sole replacement service restores your beloved footwear to like-new condition. Using premium Vibram or Italian leather soles, we carefully remove the worn sole, prepare the surface, and attach the new sole with precision stitching or bonding. Each pair undergoes quality inspection before return.

What's Included

  • Premium Vibram or Italian leather soles
  • Full sole and heel replacement
  • Original stitch pattern matching
  • Edge finishing and polishing
  • 6-month workmanship warranty

Process

We start with a thorough inspection of your shoes. Then we carefully remove the old sole, prepare the welt or bonding surface, and attach the new premium sole. Finally, we edge finish, polish, and perform quality control checks before returning your restored shoes.

Turnaround Time

Standard service: 5-7 business days. Express service available for an additional fee (2-3 business days).
