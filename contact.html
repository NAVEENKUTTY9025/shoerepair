Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our mobile shoe repair service.

How does your pickup service work?

Simply book online or call us. We'll schedule a free pickup from your home or office. Our courier will arrive with protective packaging to safely transport your items to our workshop.

How long does restoration take?

Standard repairs take 3-5 business days. Complex restorations like custom patina or major leather repairs may take 7-10 days. We offer rush service for urgent needs.

Are my items insured?

Yes! All items in our care are fully insured against loss or damage during transit and while in our workshop. Your peace of mind is our priority.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Payment is collected upon delivery of your restored items.

Do you repair all types of shoes?

Yes! We repair dress shoes, boots, sneakers, loafers, heels, and more. From luxury brands to everyday footwear, our artisans handle all materials and styles.

What's your satisfaction guarantee?

We stand behind every repair. If you're not completely satisfied with our work, we'll make it right at no additional cost or provide a full refund.
